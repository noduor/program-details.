Event Overview

The Fortune Generation Members Chinese Business Delegation to Kenya is a high-level, invitation-only business forum designed to facilitate structured engagement between Chinese investors and Kenyan public- and private-sector stakeholders.

The program is curated to move beyond introductions into practical partnership formation, investment facilitation, and long-term collaboration across priority sectors including manufacturing, energy, infrastructure, agribusiness, technology, education, and industrial development.
